Hardware

We stock the Digital Persona U.are.U 4500 a silicon optical fingerprint reader which is a market leader in terms of biometric solutions. A USB fingerprint reader with a sleek design and performance and excellence that Digital Persona is known for.

Features

  • Blue LED
  • Superior ESD resistance
  • Encrypted fingerprint data
  • Latent print rejection
  • Counterfeit finger rejection
  • Rotation invariant
  • Compatible with most U.areU SDKs

Why do I need a silicon optical fingerprint reader?

  • Silicon fingerprint readers offer a much smaller footprint, profile and lighter weight than usual fingerprint readers.
  • Optical fingerprint readers offer excellent image quality, large capture areas and impressive capture speed.
  • The silicon membrane on our fingerprint readers provide superior support for a variety of skin conditions, making them ideal for dry, moist or rough fingerprints.
  • This translates to efficent, easy and high-quality image capture for all commercial applications.
